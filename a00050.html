Detailed Description

State of the particle: ID, energy, position, direction.

The ParticleState defines the state of an ultra-high energy cosmic ray, which is assumed to be travelling at the exact speed of light. The cosmic ray state is defined by particle ID, energy and position and direction vector. For faster lookup mass and charge of the particle are stored as members.
