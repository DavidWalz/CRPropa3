Detailed Description

Deactivates the candidate beyond a maximum trajectory length.

This modules deactivates the candidate at a given maximum trajectory length. In that case the property ("Deactivated", module::description) is set. It also limits the candidates next step size to ensure the maximum trajectory length is no exceeded.
